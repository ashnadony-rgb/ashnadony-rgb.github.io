02 — About Me

Designer who codes,
thinker who ships.

I'm Ashna — a UI/UX designer with 4 years of experience turning complex problems into elegant digital products. My work sits at the intersection of research, systems thinking, and visual craft.

I believe great design is invisible. When an interface disappears and users just accomplish their goals, that's the win. I've worked with startups and scale-ups across fintech, health, and e-commerce.

Outside of pixels, I'm obsessed with typography, design history, and making tools that people actually enjoy using day to day.
